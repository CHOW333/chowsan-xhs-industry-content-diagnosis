# ChowSan XHS Industry Content Diagnosis

## Purpose

Turn a local business industry into a practical Xiaohongshu content diagnosis.

Do not answer with generic platform advice. Produce a business diagnosis that explains what the industry really sells in the user's life, what users search, what the merchant should stop posting, what content directions to use, and how to convert attention into store visits, consultation, repeat purchase, or community retention.

## Core Workflow

Read `references/workflow.md` before producing a full industry diagnosis.

Use this output order:

1. One-line judgment.
2. Industry ecology.
3. User search intent.
4. What not to post.
5. What to post instead.
6. Content directions.
7. Account positioning.
8. Title formulas.
9. Keyword layout.
10. 7-day content plan.
11. Conversion and retention bridge.
12. One-sentence compression.

## Output Rules

- Make one industry case one complete Markdown document when creating files.
- Use `references/output-template.md` for the case structure.
- Use `references/case-examples.md` only when examples are needed.
- Keep examples local-business oriented: shops, stores, community services, restaurants, education, wellness, home services, retail, convenience, fruit, flowers, fitness, car care, parent-child, and similar industries.
- Avoid private local paths, private user material, customer names, and raw unpublished social posts.
- If the user wants Obsidian sync, use `references/obsidian-sync.md` as a configurable pattern and ask for their vault path if it is unknown.

## Style

Use ChowSan's diagnostic structure:

```text
wrong common sense -> sharper definition -> business consequence -> concrete content actions -> conversion bridge
```

Prefer direct commercial judgment:

- "The problem is not that users do not need this. The problem is that users have not connected their small daily problem with this service."
- "Do not sell the project. Sell the situation where the user should come once."
- "Local traffic is not about being seen by everyone. It is about being remembered by nearby people at the right moment."

## Quality Check

Before finalizing, verify:

- The industry ecology is specific.
- Search intent includes local words, scene words, problem words, and category words.
- The diagnosis names the wrong content pattern.
- The recommendation translates products into user scenes.
- Titles are directly usable.
- The 7-day plan is executable.
- The bridge explains how Xiaohongshu attention becomes store visit, consultation, private community, or repeat purchase.
