# Case Examples

## Convenience Store

Core judgment:

> A convenience store on Xiaohongshu is not a brand-promotion account. It is a nearby instant eating and drinking decision guide.

Useful directions:

- Breakfast before work.
- Office lunch under a budget.
- Afternoon coffee and dessert.
- Late-night food after overtime.
- New arrivals and limited collaborations.
- Low-calorie combinations.
- Staff restock reminders.

## Moving Service

Core judgment:

> A moving service is not selling labor. It is reducing the user's fear that the whole process will become chaotic.

Useful directions:

- What to pack first the day before moving.
- Fees to ask before movers arrive.
- How families with children should pack.
- What causes furniture damage.
- How to avoid not finding things after moving.
